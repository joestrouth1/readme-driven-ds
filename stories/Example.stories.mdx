# Example

With `MDX` we can define stories for `Example` right in the middle of our
markdown documentation.

<Props of={Example} />

## Stories

### Singular

You can render one story at a time in a preview:

<Preview>
  <Story name="One example">
    <Example banana={false} />
  </Story>
</Preview>

Or on its own:

<Story id="mdx-example--one-example" />

### Multiple

<Preview>
  <Story name="Multiple stories">
    <div>
      <Example banana={false} />
      <Example banana={false} />
    </div>
  </Story>
</Preview>
